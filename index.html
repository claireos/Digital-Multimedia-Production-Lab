Charli XCX, our Grammys Party Princess

By Claire O'Shaughnessy

Charli XCX brought the party to the 67th Grammy Awards. Her performance began outside the venue and followed Charli as she strutted onstage in true party girl fashion, flanked by famous and fabulously-dressed friends. She gave a deliciously-raw, unpolished performance of hit songs "Von Dutch" and "Guess," dancing with her crew like no one was watching.

But who got the invite to party with Charli? Below is a (non-comprehensive list) of fellow-stars who appeared onstage with Charli at the Grammys.

  • Julia Fox
  • Alex Consani
  • Quen Blackwell
  • Gabriette
  • The Dare
  • Richie Shazam
  • Salem Mitchell
  • Greer Cohen
